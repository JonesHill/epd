Complimentary Exam for Infants

Evansville Pediatric Dentistry is committed to preventing tooth decay in children. We provide free dental examinations to children up to 18 months old in an effort to give parents (especially first-time parents) all of the information they need for their child to grow up without dental decay and to establish a Dental Home.

Parents will be provided with information concerning teething, proper diet, brushing techniques, fluoride and dental trauma. Our team will identify risk factors for dental decay and help families acheive optimal dental health for a lifetime.

Please download this form and bring into our office when you visit for a complimentary examination for children up to 18 months of age.
